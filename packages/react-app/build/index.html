Sacbe Ecosystem

Helping Communities and Investing

Human Basic Needs

Education, health, infrastructure, outdoor activities, basic needs financial funds are some of the categories where sacbe comes into play with the power and transparency of the ethereum blockchain.

Sacbe token is a crypto currency that aims to better communities, by managing simple projects. The Sacbe Portal is an easy to use ecosystem, where investors can have a say, see the process, and progress in where their money goes. With a simple vote, people can help communities in need.

With the power and technology of the Ethereum blockchain, our system allows people the ability to help fulfill basic human needs in an array of communities.

How it works?

Scouting - Planning - Development - Delivery

Our sacbe scout agents are one of the main core of the project, their responsability is to find communities with an infrastructure need.

Investors and Donors

When investing or making a donation to a project, you will receive Sacbe tokens in return. Also, everything will be recorded and managed in the blockchain. This includes transactions and the teams that will be contracted out to fulfill our mission.
