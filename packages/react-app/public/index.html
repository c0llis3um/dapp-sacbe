Sacbe Ecosystem

Helping Communities and Investing

Human Basic Needs

Education, health, infrastructure, outdoor activities, basic needs financial funds are some of the categories where sacbe comes into play with the power and transparency of the polygon blockchain.

Sacbe token is a crypto currency that aims to better communities, by managing simple projects. The Sacbe Portal is an easy to use ecosystem, where investors can have a say, see the process, and progress in where their money goes. With a simple vote, people can help communities in need.

With the power and technology of the Polygon blockchain, our system allows people the ability to help fulfill basic human needs in an array of communities.
